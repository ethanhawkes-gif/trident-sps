Our Story

A father-son operation that earns the business every day.

Bruce and Connor Hannough launched Trident in 2019 — months before COVID tore the supply chain apart. While the industry seized up, they kept food processors and cold-chain operators supplied. That trial set the standard they still run by.

Bruce leads sales and client relationships with 30-plus years in perishable protein. Connor runs operations, logistics, and the infrastructure that keeps 65+ accounts moving across the country.

"Build something durable, stay close to your clients, and never stop earning the business."— The Trident operating principle

Sustainable by Design

Reusable isn't a feature. It's the whole point.

Based in a fishing community, we've seen what disposable packaging does to the water. Every container we put into service is engineered to last for years — and responsibly repurposed at the end of its life, keeping plastic out of landfills and out of the ocean.
